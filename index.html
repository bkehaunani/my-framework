The Web should work for you.

Everybody has an online presence these days, but simply having a website is not enough. Your webpage should help you meet your goals, whether that is to sell a product, share information, or foster an online community. So is your website working for you? If you need to retool your website (or establish one in the first place), I can help.

Quality content is what’s important.

Don’t you just hate it when you can’t find what you’re looking for on the web? Your visitors feel the same way. Irrelevant content, unintuitive navigation and usability problems are a sure-fire way to lose visitors, and potential customers from your site. I believe that quality content and usability are a website’s most important features.

Make access easy for everyone.

Web users come in all shapes, sizes, and ability levels. They are also using a wide variety of devices to connect to the internet these days. That’s why accessibility and responsive designs are more important than ever. I’ll make sure your site is a pleasure to use for everyone, whether they’re using assistive technology like screen readers, a mobile device or their desktop computer to connect with you.
